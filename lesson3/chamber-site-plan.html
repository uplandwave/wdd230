This is my site plan where all the planning will happen so you can see it

Site Name

Holly Springs Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

This site will serve as a centralized hub for businesses in Holly Springs. Providing a place for networking and collaboration. Local businesses can connect, share resources, and explore potential partnerships on this site if they want to.

This site will also help other businesses find out what is already in our town. This shows off our businesses and will help keep our town's reputation up.

Scenarios

Scenario 1 If someone someday thought "Oh man I really wish i knew what businesses were growing in Holly Springs?" They could easily hop on our sight and find new and current data answering their question.

Scenario 2 Let's say you just started your very own business and because you are not smart and your business stinks you could get on our site and add your business to our chamber of Commerce getting you new information and connections that you could use to help your business

Scenario 3 In a grown up fast pace world where everyone is more concerned with their phone battery percentage rather than touching grass meeting new people is hard. This is where we come in. Right on our site we will have a place for you to learn about people actually doing things. You could meet our directors or get in contact with business owners. It all stats when you want to.
