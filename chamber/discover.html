Jackson Hole Chamber of Commerce History:

The Jackson Hole Chamber of Commerce has been a cornerstone of the community since its inception, serving as a vital hub for local businesses and tourism development. Founded to promote economic growth and preserve the unique character of Jackson Hole, the Chamber has played a pivotal role in shaping the region's identity and fostering a vibrant economy.

Key Milestones

  • 1921: Formation of the Jackson Hole Chamber of Commerce
  • 1950s: Establishment of the Jackson Hole Winter Carnival
  • 1970: Launch of the Old West Days celebration
  • 1987: Opening of the Jackson Hole & Greater Yellowstone Visitor Center
  • 1996: Introduction of the Sustainable Business Program
  • 2010: Launch of the Jackson Hole EcoTour Adventures

These milestones reflect the Chamber's enduring dedication to promoting Jackson Hole's economic growth, preserving its natural beauty, and celebrating its rich cultural heritage.
